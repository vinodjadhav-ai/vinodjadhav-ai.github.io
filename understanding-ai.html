Understanding Artificial Intelligence

1. Module Overview

This module introduced the foundations of Artificial Intelligence and helped me understand how AI is applied across different domains. It provided an overview of core AI concepts, including intelligent systems, machine learning, data-driven decision-making, ethics, automation, and the wider impact of AI on society and professional practice.

As a professional with a background in enterprise infrastructure and wireless networking, this module helped me begin connecting my existing technical experience with new AI concepts. It also encouraged me to think about how AI can be used responsibly in real-world environments such as intelligent infrastructure, automation, predictive analytics, and decision-support systems.

2. Learning Outcomes

  • Develop a foundational understanding of Artificial Intelligence and its role in modern computing.
  • Understand how AI systems can learn from data and support decision-making.
  • Recognise the importance of ethical, legal, social, and professional issues in AI development and deployment.
  • Explore the relationship between AI, machine learning, automation, and intelligent systems.
  • Reflect on how AI can be applied to professional and real-world problems.

6. Reflection

What?

In this module, I was introduced to the broader field of Artificial Intelligence, including how intelligent systems are designed, how machines can learn from data, and how AI is increasingly influencing industries and society. This was an important starting point for me because I came from a strong networking and infrastructure background rather than a software or data science background.

So What?

The module helped me realise that AI is not only about algorithms or programming. It also involves problem definition, data quality, ethical responsibility, decision-making, and understanding the wider impact of technology. I found this valuable because it allowed me to connect AI concepts with my professional experience in enterprise networking, where automation and intelligent monitoring are becoming increasingly important.

One key learning point was that successful AI adoption depends not only on technical capability, but also on responsible design, appropriate use of data, and awareness of social and professional implications.

What Next?

Going forward, I aim to build stronger practical knowledge in machine learning, data analysis, Python programming, and AI-driven automation. I also want to explore how AI can be applied to wireless networking, predictive troubleshooting, intelligent monitoring, and network automation use cases.
