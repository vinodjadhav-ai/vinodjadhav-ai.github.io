Professional Skills Matrix

This skills matrix records my professional development during the MSc Artificial Intelligence programme. It helps me reflect on my current strengths, areas for improvement, evidence of learning, and future action plan.

Skills Matrix

Skill Area Current Level Evidence / Example Action Plan
Critical Thinking Developing Analysing academic literature, discussion posts, and AI-related case studies. Continue improving by comparing different viewpoints and supporting arguments with references.
Research Skills Developing Reading academic papers and applying Harvard referencing in module submissions. Improve literature search, source evaluation, and academic writing confidence.
Communication Good Writing discussion posts, peer responses, reflections, and professional explanations. Continue improving clarity, academic tone, and structured writing.

Problem Solving Good Using professional networking experience to approach technical and analytical problems. Apply structured problem-solving methods to AI and data science tasks.

Reflective Practice Developing Using reflective writing to assess learning, challenges, strengths, and next steps. Use the What, So What, What Next model consistently across modules.

Personal Development Action Plan

  • Improve confidence in Python and R through weekly practical exercises.
  • Develop stronger academic writing by reading more peer-reviewed research papers.
  • Build small AI and data analysis projects and document them in this e-portfolio.
  • Improve critical reflection by linking learning experiences with professional practice.
  • Explore how AI can be applied to wireless networking, predictive automation, and intelligent infrastructure.

Reflection

This skills matrix has helped me identify that my strongest foundation is my professional experience in enterprise networking and technical problem-solving. However, I am still developing confidence in academic research, programming, statistical analysis, and AI implementation.

My main development priority is to combine my existing infrastructure knowledge with new AI capabilities. This will allow me to move towards AI-driven network automation, predictive analytics, and intelligent systems in my future professional work.
