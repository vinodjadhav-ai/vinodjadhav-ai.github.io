Launch Into Computing

1. Module Overview

This module introduced the fundamental concepts of computing, digital technologies, professional practice, and academic learning skills required for postgraduate study. It provided the foundation for understanding modern computing environments, problem-solving approaches, and the role of technology in society.

2. Learning Outcomes

  • Develop foundational understanding of computing concepts and digital systems.
  • Understand professional, ethical, and social responsibilities in computing.
  • Develop academic writing, research, and communication skills.
  • Improve confidence in online learning and collaborative working environments.
  • Reflect on personal and professional development throughout the module.

6. Reflection: What? So What? What Next?

What?

This module helped me transition into postgraduate computing studies by introducing the foundations of computing, academic learning, professional practice, and reflective development.

So What?

Coming from a strong infrastructure and networking background, the module helped me adjust to academic learning, structured reflection, research expectations, and collaborative online study.

What Next?

I plan to continue strengthening my academic writing, research, and technical skills while building confidence in Artificial Intelligence, programming, and data-driven problem-solving.
