Research Methods and Professional Practice

1. Module Overview

This module focuses on professional, legal, social, cultural, and ethical issues in computing, together with academic research methods. It supports the development of research questions, literature evaluation, methodology selection, data analysis, and research proposal writing.

2. Learning Outcomes

  • Appraise professional, legal, social, cultural, and ethical issues affecting computing professionals.
  • Apply principles of academic investigation to a research topic in computing.
  • Evaluate existing literature, research design, methodology, and data analysis processes.
  • Produce and critically evaluate a research proposal.

3. Artefacts

  • Collaborative discussion forum summaries
  • Research proposal draft and final submission
  • Literature review notes
  • Statistical analysis activities
  • Professional practice reflections
  • Feedback from peers and tutors

What?
