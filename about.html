About Me

Welcome! I am Vinod Jadhav, an experienced enterprise infrastructure professional currently pursuing an MSc in Artificial Intelligence. My goal is to bridge the gap between traditional network infrastructure and modern intelligent, data-driven systems.

With over 18 years of experience in Wireless Networking and Cisco technologies, I have worked extensively on designing, deploying, troubleshooting, and optimizing enterprise network environments.

My growing interest in Artificial Intelligence and automation has inspired me to explore intelligent systems, machine learning, predictive analytics, and AI-driven network automation.

This e-portfolio showcases my academic learning, reflective practice, technical artefacts, professional development, and continuous learning journey throughout the MSc Artificial Intelligence programme.
